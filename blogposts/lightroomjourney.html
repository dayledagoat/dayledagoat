My Lightroom Journey

Published on June 21, 2026 by Dayle | Category: Journal

Well, if you know know me, you may also know I love photography. You may also know that I love shooting at different scenic locations, trying to get the best shot imaginable. Last week, I went to New York to take pictures

Me and a couple friends did a "Triangular Trip", starting at the World Trade Center, taking the ferry to midtown, and eventually making our way back to 14th.

Once the fun part was over, I had to do the dreaded task: Editing all the pictures :O

In short, I misjudged how many pictures I truly had, and I spent way more time than I'd like to admit between sorting and editing all the pictures. End result was way worth it tho. B)
