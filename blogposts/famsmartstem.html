The Comeback: Phase 1

Published on June 18, 2026 by Dayle | Category: Journal

What most of you may not know is that I used to co-run a Stem Based Youtube channel. Well, I don't blame you for not knowing after we went on a year long hiatus of no uploads(really sorry :c)

We got too caught up in the hype of trying to be famous online, and in our class, that we burnt ourselves out with the daily upload schedule we had. Now that that hype is over, I was thinking of using my channel as a personal passion project to help me study and learn in the process.

I have big plans for this channel, and I want to take it into a new direction. Since I'll be busy my senior year, I'll limit the uplods to three times a week just so I don't accidentally ghost my channel again

Over the summer, I'll be more consistent with the channel, making videos based on what I've learned this year in Pre-Calc, ranging from Trig and the identities, the Unit Circle, rational functions, and different types of graphs.
